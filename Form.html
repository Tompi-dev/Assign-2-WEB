About Olivia Rodrigo

Olivia Rodrigo (born February 20, 2003) is an American singer-songwriter and actress. She rose to fame with her acting roles on Disney shows before achieving global recognition with her debut single “drivers license” in 2021. Known for her emotional lyrics and powerful vocals, Rodrigo has quickly become one of the defining pop stars of her generation.
